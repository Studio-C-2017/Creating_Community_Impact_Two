Employers

Creating Community Impact specializes in working with employers to identify ways to make their workplace more efficient by saving them time which saves them money. We assist employers to look at a different way of hiring employees as well as a different type of job classification. The hours can range from as few as 2 hours per week to 20 hours or even full time. The process used is customized to your business needs. The services include training and ongoing support provided by Creating Community Impact employee specialists at no cost to your business at whatever level and time frame is needed. Employees who can provide more efficiency for the workplace by redistributing the work load or changing the way the employer looks at the position have been shown to provide an optimum environment for your businessâ€™ efficiency. Your skilled employees can be unburdened from the tasks that are not necessary for them to do and this will better leverage this expensive resource. Removing tedious activities from skilled employees improves their job satisfaction and contributes to employee retention. By looking at the employers needs creatively great things can happen. You will have an employee who is thrilled to come to work every day, feels accomplished and proud of their work. As an employer who hires individuals with disabilities you will gain a sense of stewardship toward the community you work and live in. Additionally you will find your workforce is more diversified, proud of themselves and the business they work for.
